Check the satisfaction of equilibrium expressions and conservation laws.

  • c0 (array_like, dict, Series, or DataFrame, shape (n_points, n_compounds) or (n_compounds, ), or None) â Each row contains the total âinitialâ concentration of all possible chemical species in solution.
